About Us


With social economy development, people are more and more requiring tourism. Therefore, the tourist industry should rapidly develop.
As various tourist information increases, it becomes more difficult to conveniently and quickly acquire the appropriate information.
Scenic spot recommendation in personalized tourism can effectively solve the information overloading so that the industry of tourism sustainably and healthily develops.

Access to relevant and accurate information is at the heart of tourism.
Overload has become such a serious issue for those seeking for appropriate information.
Various researches have been carried out on how to make information on tourism website more effective.
Intelligent globetrotting information system tries to bridge the gap between tourist and useful information. It does this by noting what a tourist perceives as relevant, in terms of personalized content pertaining to tourism products. It then shows it in this tourism websites.
